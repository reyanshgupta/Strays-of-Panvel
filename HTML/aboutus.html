Frequently Asked Questions:

How do I adopt a dog?

To adopt a dog, please fill out our adoption application and our adoption team will be in touch with you.

What is the adoption fee?

Our adoption fee is $200 per dog..

Do you have dogs for people with allergies?

We cannot guarantee that any of our dogs will be hypoallergenic, but we do have some dogs that are less likely to cause an allergic reaction.
